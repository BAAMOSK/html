Speak Another Language Using Words You Already Know

3 Easy Steps!

  1. Start with words you know.
  2. Learn only the most spoken words.
  3. Practice by having a conversation.

STEP 1

Use words from a familiar language.

Hoe the most famous of gardening tools.

Chalk the porous carbonate rock used for writing or eating.

Hoe + chalk = 'very cool'. Congratulations you now know how to speak Cantonese.

STEP 2

Learn the 1000 most common words using easy to remember mnemonics. No need to memorize a dictionary.

This is hay. Sit on it or eat it if you're a horse.

Did you know that fay is a fairy? Google it if you don't believe me.

This fay on the hay told you to get back to studying. Because it's the boss! You just said boss in Spanish.

STEP 3

Next step is to practice. Have a conversation with another person trying to learn.

Use the chat to leave voice messages to a complete stranger.

Get help with your pronunciation and accent.

You scratch their back they scratch yours. It's a never-ending win-win situation.
